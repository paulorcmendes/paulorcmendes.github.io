Bio

I am currently a researcher at TeleMedia Lab and Master Student in Informatics at the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio). Graduated in Computer Science from the Federal University of Maranhão (UFMA). I've been awarded several times in knowledge competitions, such as the Brazilian Public Schools Mathematics Olympiad - OBMEP and the Brazilian Astronomy and Astronautics Olympiad - OBA.
